Baxley is one of the poorest worlds in the Federated Suns and also has the worst education system as well. The planet is primarily agrarian, however crops yield barely enough to sustain the planet and the only other industry on planet involves mining the modest mineral deposits of Baxley. The planet is primarily arid and only has a small band capable of sustaining human life.

Due to the lack of education and wealth, the population in the early 31st Century had a great reverence for ComStar and the planet had a high rate of the population willing to join the order.
